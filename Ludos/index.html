Our Story

Give your taste buds a treat at Ludo's.

Proud to continue its French origin and reputation as the Central Coast's premier patisserie, executive chef and owner of Ludo's Gourmet Kitchen, as it is known today, Peter and his wife Rita have taken the business to the next level with their vision to deliver the ultimate sweet and savoury gourmet experiences for people living, working or holidaying on the Central Coast.

Food and shared experiences are our passion and we believe that this passion is evident in every gourmet product and meal we create.

The Ludo's Patisserie Vision

As Central Coast locals, we are committed to offering the very best to those we share this incredible community with. We are proud to support our local farmers by sourcing their freshest seasonal produce daily to deliver the ultimate gourmet experience for you to enjoy on your own, with family, friends or colleagues, anywhere any time. In order to deliver this vision we have grown our business to offer three distinctive services, with all products and meals within each arm of our business hand-made on our premises by our team of professional chefs and bakers from premium locally sourced produce and ingredients.
